I was born and raised in India, but I moved to America at 21 for work and education. It still feels surreal, unbelievable and crazy that someone actually flew me out to work in the states. Since then, I've had the chance to visit some incredible places, and one of the most memorable was New York City. I took amazing photos during my visit, and ever since, I've felt that spending my twenties in New York would be an incredible experience.
